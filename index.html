🖊️ About the Author

Sumaiya writes with the guts of a survivor and the heart of a poet. Born from silence, her work speaks of longing, resilience, and the messy beauty of memory. Whether she’s writing letters to the dead or poems from the softest places in her chest, her words are here to remind you: you are not alone.
